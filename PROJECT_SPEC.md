# IPL AI Auction Simulator

## Objective

Build a multi-agent IPL auction simulation where AI-controlled franchises bid on players and construct squads.

## Teams

- MI
- CSK
- RCB
- RR
- KKR
- GT
- LSG
- PBKS
- DC
- SRH

## Inputs

- Player Name
- Age
- Nationality
- Role
- Runs
- Wickets
- Strike Rate
- Economy Rate
- Auction Price

## Outputs

- Auction Results
- Final Squads
- ROI Analysis
- Team Rankings

## Research Questions

1. Which franchise strategy produces the highest ROI?
2. Which players are consistently overvalued?
3. Which players are consistently undervalued?
4. How do AI decisions compare with real IPL auction outcomes?
